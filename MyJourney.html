My First Dream

My first dream is to become a doctor because I want to help people and save lives. I admire how doctors care for their patients and bring hope to those who are suffering. Someday, I hope to wear a white coat and serve others with kindness and compassion.

Challenges I Faced

Because of financial challenges, my first dream of becoming a doctor changed. I realized that pursuing medicine required a lot of money, which my family couldn’t afford. Even though it was difficult, I learned to accept reality and find a new dream that still allows me to help others in a different way.

My Achievements

Despite not reaching my first dream, I still appreciate my achievements in junior high. I was able to do well in my studies, join school activities, and grow as a person. These accomplishments remind me that success is not only about one dream but also about the effort and growth I gained along the way.

What’s Next

What I decided to do next was to follow my strengths and pursue ICT. I discovered that I enjoy working with computers and technology, which helps me express my creativity and problem-solving skills. Even though my path changed, I believe ICT will lead me to a future where I can still make a difference.

My Steps in Achieving my Dream

Now, I truly enjoy my studies because I’m learning something I love. Studying ICT makes me feel motivated and excited to improve my skills. I realized that following my passion leads to both happiness and success.

My Realization

My realization is that dreams can change, and that’s okay. What matters most is discovering my true strengths and using them to build a meaningful future. I learned that every challenge has a purpose and can lead me to the path where I truly belong.
